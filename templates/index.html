About LeafAI

Empowering Agritech

Our Mission

LeafAI is an industry-ready agricultural platform built to help farmers, gardeners, and agronomists quickly detect crop diseases using deep learning. By identifying diseases like Early Blight, Late Blight, and Spider Mites instantly, we aim to reduce crop yield losses and promote sustainable farming practices.

How it Works

Our backend runs on a highly optimized Flask architecture communicating with a TensorFlow/Keras neural network. When a user uploads a photo, the image is passed into our prediction service which calculates the specific disease class alongside a confidence threshold logic that rejects non-plant images. Finally, the results are cross-referenced with our comprehensive Knowledge Base mapping the disease to specific Organic and Chemical treatments.
